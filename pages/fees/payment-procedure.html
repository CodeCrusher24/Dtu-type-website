Payment Procedure for Hostel & Mess Fee

Instructions to pay Hostel & Mess Fees as per allotted seat:

Students can pay their Hostel Fee online by checking their allotment status in the link given on Hostel website during Hostel Allotment process.

To make the mess fee:

  • Open the given link on hostel website.
  • Search and select the following mess group account as per your allotted hostel:
    • "Group A Mess DTU" (for HJB, SVS, APJ & BCH)
    • "Group B Mess DTU" (for CVR, JCB & VMH)
    • "Group C Mess DTU" (for SNH, VLB & KCH)
    • "Group D Mess DTU" (for ABH, NDPG, Type-2 & Ramanujan (Transit) Hostel)
  • Enter your personal and payment details and complete the process.

If someone finds any problem in making payment of mess bill, he/she may contact Hostel Office for disbursement of his/her query.
